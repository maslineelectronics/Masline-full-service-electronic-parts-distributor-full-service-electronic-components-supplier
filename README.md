## Quality Assurance and Verification

Quality control defines the reputation of a full service distributor. Each component passes through detailed testing and verification stages to ensure compliance with international standards and customer specifications.

### Testing Protocols Include:
1. **Visual and microscopic inspection** to detect labeling inconsistencies.  
2. **Electrical parameter testing** for performance validation.  
3. **X-ray and decapsulation analysis** for internal structure verification.  
4. **Functional testing** for in-circuit and out-of-circuit performance.  
5. **Moisture and ESD protection** to maintain handling integrity.  

All inspection and test results are documented, ensuring traceability for every shipped component.
